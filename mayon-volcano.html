Mayon Volcano

Explore the majestic Mayon Volcano.

Mayon Volcano is an active stratovolcano located in the province of Albay in the Bicol Region, Philippines. It is renowned for its perfect cone shape and is considered one of the most picturesque volcanoes in the world.

The volcano is part of the Pacific Ring of Fire and has erupted over 50 times in the past 400 years. Its most destructive eruption occurred in 1814, burying several towns under volcanic debris and lava flows.

Visitors to Mayon Volcano can enjoy activities such as hiking, sightseeing, and exploring the surrounding natural beauty. The area offers stunning views of the volcano, its lush vegetation, and nearby landmarks.
