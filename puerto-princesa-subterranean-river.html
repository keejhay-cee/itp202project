Puerto-Princesa Subterranean River

Explore the wonders of the Puerto-Princesa Subterranean River.

The Puerto-Princesa Subterranean River National Park is located about 80 kilometers north of the city of Puerto Princesa, Palawan, Philippines. It features a limestone karst mountain landscape and is known for its long navigable underground river.

The river is 8.2 kilometers long and flows directly into the West Philippine Sea. Its underground section features major formations of stalactites and stalagmites, and several large chambers.

Visitors to the Puerto-Princesa Subterranean River can enjoy activities such as guided boat tours through the river, hiking through the lush forest, and wildlife watching.
