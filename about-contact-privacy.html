About Us

Welcome to Pasyal Pilipinas

We are a group of passionate students from Asians Development Foundation College, currently in our 1st year of Bachelor of Science in Information Technology. Our team is composed of four dedicated members: Kinneth Jake Custodio, Rosenie Facturanan, Trixie Marcha, and Bebita Trangia.

Our Mission

Our mission is to highlight and celebrate the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of the Philippines. Through our website, we aim to provide a comprehensive guide to the countryâ€™s most stunning destinations, historical landmarks, and cultural treasures.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the Wonders of the Philippines are known and appreciated by people from all corners of the globe. We hope to inspire travel, cultural exchange, and a deeper appreciation for our country's unique heritage.

Our Goal

The primary goal of our website is to showcase the Wonders of the Philippines. We want to create an engaging and informative platform that will serve as a go-to resource for anyone interested in exploring the diverse and beautiful landscapes of our homeland.

Meet the Team

  • Kinneth Jake Custodio: A tech enthusiast with a keen eye for detail, Kinneth is our lead web developer.
  • Rosenie Facturanan: Passionate about design, Rosenie ensures our website is visually appealing and user-friendly.
  • Trixie Marcha: With a talent for writing, Trixie brings the stories and histories of the Philippines to life through engaging content.
  • Bebita Trangia: Bebita's organizational skills keep our project on track, ensuring we meet our deadlines and goals.

Why We Do It

We believe that the Philippines has so much to offer, from its pristine beaches and majestic mountains to its vibrant festivals and warm, hospitable people. Our website is a labor of love, born out of our collective desire to share the wonders of our country with the world.

Thank you for visiting our site. We hope you enjoy exploring the Wonders of the Philippines as much as we enjoy bringing them to you.
