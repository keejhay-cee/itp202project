Hinatuan Enchanted River

Discover the magic of the Hinatuan Enchanted River.

The Hinatuan Enchanted River, located on the island of Mindanao in the Philippines, is famous for its deep blue water and mesmerizing beauty. This saltwater river is fed by underground springs, giving it an enchanting and crystal-clear appearance.

Surrounded by lush greenery and rocky riverbanks, the Enchanted River is a popular spot for tourists looking to swim in its clear waters or enjoy a serene boat ride. Local folklore tells stories of the river being enchanted by spirits, adding to its mystical charm.

Visitors to Hinatuan Enchanted River can also explore nearby attractions such as the Sibadan Fish Cage, the Hinatuan Marine Sanctuary, and various caves and waterfalls in the region.
